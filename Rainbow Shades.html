The Rainbow Shades

She was like the rainbow shades,
VIBGYOR and then, the enigma fades.
I saw her robing every day,
Orange to pink tint on her cheeks,
Disrobed, still the rose stays.

Unseen, untouched, her beauty plays,
Unknown rhythm, her mermaid sways,
High held head, her aura slays,
My curious stare to her virgin gaze.

A sacred lily in my greens,
Wandering-exploring, the curious teens,
Her fragrance melding with my veins,
To taste her charm, min heart leans,
More than seven, now, she seems.

In her blues, then my soul dips,
A soft twitch on her honey lips,
Little glitch. Oh! The drape slips,
Coming closer. Tighter grip.
Upside down, everything flips.

I see her all, still I know her not.
Leaves me always, lost in thought.
Oh! The VIBGYOR, it veils a lot,
Hoards it all so, un-adored,
Remains her beauty, unexplored.
